## Description

StatMate is a free statistics calculator that runs 20 common tests and formats results in APA 7th edition style — ready to paste into your paper.

**Why it exists:** Researchers spend hours manually formatting statistics tables. SPSS costs $99/mo. R has a steep learning curve. StatMate gives you publication-ready APA output in seconds, for free.

**What it does:**
- 20 calculators: t-test, ANOVA, chi-square, correlation, regression, factor analysis, and more
- Instant APA 7th edition tables — copy to clipboard or export as PDF/Word
- AI-powered result interpretation (Pro) — explains what your numbers actually mean
- Built-in assumption checks, effect sizes, and post-hoc analyses
- CSV/TSV upload for your existing data
- Privacy-first: all calculations run in your browser. No data uploaded.
- Trilingual: English, Korean, Japanese

**Who it's for:** Graduate students, researchers, and academics who need fast, accurate statistical analysis with publication-ready output.

**Built with:** Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS. Cross-validated against R 4.3 for accuracy.
